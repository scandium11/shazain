Samsung Galaxy S9

The Galaxy S9 and S9+ have nearly identical features to the S8 batch, with the same display size and aspect ratio, just like their predecessor. One highly regarded change to distinguish between the models is the location of the fingerprint sensor. While the S8's is found beside the camera, the S9's is directly underneath it. Samsung Galaxy S9 plus

The Samsung Galaxy S9 Plus mobile features a 6.2" (15.75 cm) display with a screen resolution of 1440 x 2960 pixels and runs on Android v8.0 (Oreo) operating system.
